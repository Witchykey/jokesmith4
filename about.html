About JokeSmith

How It Works

JokeSmith is a web application that uses OpenAI's powerful language models to generate custom jokes based on your input. Just pick a joke type, enter a topic, and let the AI do the rest!

Features

  • Dad jokes, puns, one-liners, and more
  • Custom topic-based joke generation
  • Copy and share jokes easily
  • Mobile-friendly responsive design

Technology

  • OpenAI API — AI-generated jokes
  • Cloudflare Pages + Workers — Hosting and backend logic
  • HTML, JS, Bootstrap — Clean frontend

Privacy

JokeSmith does not store your data. All interactions are private and temporary — we never retain your jokes or inputs.

Disclaimer

All jokes are generated by AI. While we aim for fun and family-friendly content, we can’t guarantee suitability. If a joke seems off, just try a new topic!
